The Mirus Initiative

What is the Mirus Initiative?

The Mirus Initiative is a program intended to embrace, encourage, and enhance individuals across a spectrum of philosophies, identities, and perspectives.

Currently, the program, ran by Sam Aster, works locally in the Montrose, Colorado area, offering the following services:

  • Peer Support
  • Neighborly Assistance
  • General Technical Assistance for PC, Android, and network devices
  • Casual Web services
  • Recommendations for services not included above
Thank you for visiting the Mirus Initiative!
